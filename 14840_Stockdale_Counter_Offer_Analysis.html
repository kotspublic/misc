4. Contingency Analysis

L(1) Loan Contingency — 10 Days  Risk: Low

With a fully underwritten loan approval already in hand, removing the loan contingency within 10 days is straightforward. The written loan commitment can be delivered to the seller within 1–3 days of acceptance.

✅
Action: Have your lender prepare the written loan commitment letter immediately upon acceptance. Deliver to seller's agent within 3 days.

L(2) Appraisal Contingency — 10 Days  Risk: Moderate

Even with a fully underwritten loan, the appraisal must be ordered, completed, and the report received within 10 days. Typical LA County timeline: appraiser scheduling 2–4 days + report delivery 3–5 days = 5–9 days total. Tight but feasible if ordered immediately.

Critical risk: If the property appraises below $720,000 (possible given as-is condition), you must receive the report and exercise your cancellation right within 10 days.

⚠️
Action: Order the appraisal on Day 1 of acceptance. Coordinate with your lender to expedite.

L(3) Investigation of Property — 10 Days  Risk: Moderate

Ten days for property investigation is tight but manageable with advance preparation. Required inspections:

  • General home inspection (schedule Day 1–2)
  • Electrician inspection — identify specific panel brand and assess safety (schedule Day 1–2)
  • Garage structural assessment (schedule Day 1–3)
  • Review of all reports (Days 7–10)

Key focus areas: Identify the specific push-button panel brand (Federal Pacific Stab-Lok, Zinsco, Pushmatic, or other) — this determines urgency of replacement. Also assess roof, plumbing, foundation, and HVAC.

⚠️
Action: Have inspectors identified and ready to schedule before acceptance. Contact them on Day 1.

L(4) Insurance Contingency — 10 Days  Risk: Low–Moderate

With the electrical panel clarified as a push-button breaker (not knob-and-tube), obtaining insurance is considerably more straightforward. Most standard carriers will insure a property with a push-button panel, though some may require an electrical inspection or add a modest surcharge.

For an investment/Airbnb property, a landlord policy or short-term rental policy is required. Available from carriers like Proper Insurance, Slice, CBIZ, or through specialty brokers.

ℹ️
Action: Contact 2–3 insurance brokers on Day 1. Specify investment/Airbnb property with push-button breaker panel. Request quotes within 5 days.

L(5) Seller Documents — Proposed 10 Days (Seller Wants 5)  Negotiate

The seller must deliver all disclosure documents (TDS, SPQ, NHD, Lead Disclosure, etc.) within 7 days of acceptance. If documents are delivered on Day 7, you would have only 3 days to review them under the seller's 5-day deadline. Under the proposed 10-day deadline, you have adequate time to review documents after delivery.

🔴
Hold firm on 10 days. This is a reasonable and fair ask that protects your ability to review documents properly.

L(6) Preliminary Title Report — Proposed 10 Days (Seller Wants 5)  Negotiate

The preliminary title report may not be available until Day 5–7 of escrow. A 5-day deadline could expire before you even receive it. Ten days provides adequate time to review for liens, encumbrances, easements, or title defects.

🔴
Hold firm on 10 days. The original offer's delivery-based protection is eliminated by the seller's 5-day hard deadline.
